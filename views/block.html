Blockchain Course

Blockchain Bitcoin Etherum

ITM New Wave IT Lab’s Blockchain Course will provide an overview of the structure and mechanism of Bitcoin, Ethereum, Hyperledger and Multichain Blockchain platforms. You will learn to setup your own private Blockchain and deploy smart contracts on Ethereum. Consecutively, you will also learn to deploy the business network using Hyperledger Composer and be able to setup a private Multichain environment.
